YPKBI Digital Admission Suite

End-to-end digital transformation of a multi-school admissions process. Four interconnected web apps handling 4,000+ concurrent students across proctoring, voice recording, group discussion assessment, and final selection decisions.

The Problem
How do you run a high-stakes multi-school admissions process for 4,000+ concurrent students — without paper, without chaos, and without a large ops team?
My Role
Digital Transformation Specialist · YPKBI
The Outcome
Four interconnected web apps covering online proctoring, voice-recorded spoken assessment, live group discussion scoring, and a final selection decision dashboard.
