About

I design learning experiences and build the systems that deliver them.

I'm a learning designer based in Jakarta, currently leading digital transformation at YPKBI, designing the tools, systems, and experiences that serve students, educators, and administrators across a network of schools.

Before this, I spent two years developing enterprise training at PT Nindya Karya and earned my Master's from NYU in Learning Technology and Experience Design. I'm fascinated by the intersection of pedagogy, technology, and the small design decisions that make people actually want to learn.

I also get unreasonably interested in whatever I'm working on, which is a polite way of saying I will read the entire Wikipedia rabbit hole and come back with three new questions. I think that's a feature, not a bug.
